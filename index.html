My UX Skills

User Research

User research design is the process of planning and conducting research to understand the needs, behaviors, and motivations of users within a specific context. It involves selecting appropriate methods, such as interviews, surveys, or usability testing, to gather insights that inform product development and enhance user experience.

User Interface Design

User interface design is the process of creating intuitive and aesthetically pleasing interfaces for software applications, websites, or devices, ensuring that users can interact with them effectively. It involves understanding user needs and preferences to enhance usability and overall user experience through layout, visual elements, and interactive features.

Web Development

Web development design involves creating the visual and functional aspects of websites, focusing on user experience, usability, and aesthetic appeal. It encompasses both front-end design, which deals with layout and interaction elements, and back-end development, which ensures the site operates smoothly and efficiently.
